Book Information

  • Author: Morgan Housel
  • Genre: Personal Finance, Psychology, Self-Help
  • Published: 2020

Book Summary

The Psychology of Money explores the hidden forces behind how people think about wealth, spending, and investing. Instead of focusing on complex financial theories, Morgan Housel emphasizes that financial success is less about knowledge and more about behavior. Through 19 short and engaging stories, he highlights how emotions, experiences, and biases shape our financial decisions.
